Sergio Sancho

I'm a joint PhD student at ETH Zurich and Disney Research, supervised by Markus Gross and Marios Papas. I earned my Master's degree in 2023 from ETH Zurich, and my Bachelor's degree in 2020 from the University of Valencia.

From a young age, I've been fascinated by the technical and creative aspects of filmmaking, particularly in animation. My current research interests include computer graphics, physically-based rendering and simulation, and the intersection of these fields with machine learning.
